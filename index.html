What is this?

Perspective Cards are a tool to help makers be more inclusive.

Each of these cards presents a different perspective coupled with actionable steps to help you gain new insights, challenge your bias, and (hopefully) move us one step closer toward meaningful inclusion.

Please feel free to use them, share them, remix them.
